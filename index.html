About

Find Out More About Us

TecHacks, a student-run hackathon, facilitated by Chitkara University in Punjab, was founded in 2019 by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering with 110 participants and expanded to 350 participants in 2020.

TecHacks 3.0, as a hybrid Open-source hackathon, will give participants from various technical backgrounds the opportunity to engage with a wonderful community of hackers while also providing seminars and mentorship from industry leaders.
