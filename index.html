About

Find Out More About Us

TecHacks, a student-run hackathon community, founded by the folks from ECE Department at Chitkara University , Punjab in 2019 with the motto to provide a qulity experience where young minds collaborate together to innovate things for the upliftment of the society.

TecHacks 4.0, as a in-person, Open-source hackathon, will give participants from various backgrounds the opportunity to engage with a wonderful community of hackers while also providing seminars and mentorship from industry leaders.

  • Who can participate in the hackathon?

    Everyone is welcome to apply, be it students, professionals or turing-test certified androids. As long as you wish to learn something, we'll be waiting to see you. If you are under 18 years of age, we’ll need a parental consent form.

  • Are there any fees to participate?

    No, TecHacks is free of cost and open for all.

  • Do I need a team?

    Yes! keeping overall fairness and productivity in mind, participants will be required to have teams of MAXIMUM 4 people.

  • What if I don't have a team?

    No worries, head on to the Discord channel and meet people in the #🤝team-building channel.

  • Can I bring a project that was built earlier?

    We apologize, but all the hackathon's projects should be developed during the event from scratch. All hackers will be strictly monitored for any kind of plagiarism or cheating.

  • What a facilities will bd provided to the participants?

    All the participants will be facilitated with free of cost accommodation and food services between the Opening Ceremony on 29th April and the Closing Ceremony on 1st May.
